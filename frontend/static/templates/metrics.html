Metrics
A range of metrics are calculated and used within the dashboard:
Team Rating
Used within the y-axis in the Fixtures table
Used in the calculation for the form rating metric
A quantification of a team's long-term performance. The metric is calculated by first calculating a rating for each team, for each of the last 4 seasons (including the current season). The ratings consider the a team's position, points and goal difference for each given season. A team's overall team rating is calculated by taking a weighted average of the teams individal season ratings, with a greater weighting for more recent seasons. The metric is normalised, making each rating relative to the other temas in the Premier League. If a team performs top in all of the seasons used to calculate this metric, their team rating will be 1.
Team Form Rating
Prediction
A quantification of each team's recent current performance based on the results of their last 5 games. To calculate the form rating for Team A, for each of the 5 most recent games, the absolute difference between Team A's team rating and the team rating of the opposition is multiplied by the goal difference achieved against the opposition team. For each of the 5 games if Team A plays and Team A wins: That game will add the form rating of the opposition team multiplied by the goal difference achieved towards Team A's form rating Team A draws: That game will add (or subtract if negative) the difference in Team A's form rating and the opposition team's form ratings towards Team A's form rating Team A loses: That game will subtract the form rating of the opposition team multiplied by the goal difference achieved from Team A's form rating These 5 positive or negative values for the last 5 games are summed to create the form rating. The rating is capped between 0 and 1.
